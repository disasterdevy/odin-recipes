Air Fryer Baked Potatoes

Description:

A baked potato in the air fryer comes out beautifully crusty on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside, just what a great baked potato should be!

Serve them with your favorite toppings, like sour cream, butter, or chives.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • tablespoon peanut oil
  • Â½ teaspoon coarse sea salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Brush potatoes with peanut oil, sprinkle with salt, and place them in the air fryer basket.
  3. Cook potatoes until very tender when pierced with a fork, about 1 hour.
